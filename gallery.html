Pakistan Army

The Pakistan Army is the principal land warfare branch of the Pakistan Armed Forces. The Pakistan Army's primary objective and constitutional mission is to ensure the national security and national unity of Pakistan by defending it against external aggression or the threat of war. It can also be requisitioned by the federal government to respond to internal threats by maintaining peace and security within its land borders.
